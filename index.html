About Our Bakery and Café


We offer a wide and ever changing variety of cakes from our bakery, freshly made in house using the highest quality ingredients. You'll find pretty cupcakes, delectable handcrafted petit fours and slices of our signature layer cakes waiting for you to enjoy in our café or to take away with you.

Enjoy Afternoon Tea With Us


Join us in our café to enjoy afternoon tea and sample a selection of our delicious cakes with a hot cup of tea or coffee. Choose your tasty treats from the range available in the bakery and our staff will bring them to your table served on a tiered cake stand.


There is no need to book a table however table reservations are more than welcome.

Bespoke Celebration Cakes


We create luxury wedding, celebration and event cakes to your specific requirements. Our cakes are sure to impress, created with mouth-watering flavours, expert craftsmanship and acute attention to detail. Every element of your cake will be handcrafted using the finest ingredients.


We can provide assistance selecting flavours, fillings and design elements to give you the cake of your dreams. Whether you would like an elaborate themed cake or fine detailed pipework, we will create a delicious masterpiece for your special occasion.


Get in touch to discuss your requirements.
